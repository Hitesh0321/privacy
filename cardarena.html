Privacy Policy for CardArena

Last updated: November 5, 2025

CardArena (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is a free offline card game. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile game.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information directly from you.

However, our game displays third-party advertisements provided by Unity Ads. Unity Ads may collect certain non-personal data automatically for advertising purposes, such as:

  • Device identifiers (like Advertising ID)
  • Device type, language, and operating system
  • Game activity (e.g., ad impressions or clicks)
  • Approximate location (country or region)

This information helps Unity Ads deliver relevant ads and measure performance.

Learn more here: Unity Privacy Policy

2. Offline Gameplay

Our game can be played entirely offline. When offline, no data is sent or received over the internet, except for Unity Ads if a connection is available.

3. Children’s Privacy

Our game is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

4. Third-Party Services

The only third-party service used is Unity Ads, which may collect anonymous data as described above. We do not share user data with any other third parties.

5. Data Security

We do not store or manage any personal data. Unity Ads handles its own data securely per its policies.

6. Your Choices

You can opt out of personalized ads by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
